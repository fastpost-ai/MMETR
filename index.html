About Mangozho Foundation

Mangozho Foundation is MMETR's not-for-profit arm, operating programs in education, community health, and local economic empowerment. We partner with NGOs, governments and private sponsors to deliver measurable impact.

Our Mission

The Mangozho Foundation is dedicated to improving lives and building sustainable communities through strategic investments in healthcare, education, clean water, and economic development. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic necessities and opportunities for growth.

Since our establishment in 2010, we have impacted over 2 million lives across 15 countries through our various programs and initiatives.

Healthcare Infrastructure Program

We are constructing hospitals in partnership with Unicore Group, building 12 Oncology centres in Zimbabwe for USD $523 Million. These will be distributed as follows:

  • 1 in each of the 10 provinces (Manicaland, Mashonaland Central, Mashonaland East, Mashonaland West, Masvingo, Matabeleland North, Matabeleland South, Midlands, Masvingo, and Manicaland)
  • Harare will have 2 Oncology centres
  • Bulawayo will have 2 Oncology centres

Oncology Centres Project

Partnership: Unicore Group

Investment: USD $523 Million

Centres: 12 Oncology centres across Zimbabwe

Timeline: 2024-2027

Other Programs

Clean Water Initiatives
Providing access to clean drinking water through well construction and water purification systems.

Impact: 120 water projects completed, benefiting 300,000+ people

Education Support
Scholarships, school construction, and educational resources for children in disadvantaged communities.

Impact: 15,000+ scholarships awarded, 25 schools built/renovated

Sustainable Agriculture
Training and resources for sustainable farming practices to improve food security and economic opportunities.

Impact: 5,000+ farmers trained, 50+ communities supported

About PSTQQ

PSTQQ operates across the full economic spectrum from Primary → Quaternary → Quinary sectors. Below is how we engage across each level:

Primary Sector Operations

Extraction & production of raw materials directly from the earth — agriculture, fishing, forestry, mining, and quarrying.

  • Mining Operations: Active mining operations in Gokwe and Penalonga
  • Farming Operations: Agricultural activities in Midlands and Mashonaland East

Secondary Sector

Transformation of raw materials into finished goods including manufacturing, metalworking, construction, and the production of textiles, automobiles and electronic appliances.

Tertiary Sector

Wholesale & retail trade, finance, banking, tourism, entertainment and transportation services.

Quaternary Sector

Knowledge-based activities: information technology (IT), education, research & development (R&D), and consulting services.

Quinary Sector

High-level decision making, invention, top-level research and the development of new ideas and technologies that drive economic transformation.

Strategic Partnerships

Hachtec Partnership

Civil engineering & infrastructure partnership for major highway/motorways & freeways around Zimbabwe.

Hydrogen Africa Partnership

Green energy power solutions through our partnership with Hydrogen Africa.
